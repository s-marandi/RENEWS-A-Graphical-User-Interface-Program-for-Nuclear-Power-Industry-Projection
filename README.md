**RENEWS (Reactor Expansion & Nuclear Employment Workforce Simulator)** 

a graphical simulation tool that links nuclear reactor deployment with fuel cycle material demand and workforce requirements.

Developed at the University of Maryland, the tool helps stakeholders explore “what-if” scenarios for nuclear growth in the U.S., using Monte Carlo simulation to account for uncertainty. With a simple interface, RENEWS allows users to test assumptions on:
License extensions for existing reactors
Deployment of advanced reactors under different growth patterns
Fuel cycle demand (mining, milling, conversion, enrichment, fabrication)
Operations and workforce needs across the industry
The tool is designed for decision support and education—helping policymakers, industry, and researchers visualize how technology expansion, workforce pipelines, and supply chain readiness interact in the path toward 2050 clean energy goals.
